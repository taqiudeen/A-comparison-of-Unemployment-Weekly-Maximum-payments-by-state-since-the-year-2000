A comparison of Unemployment Weekly Maximum payments by state since the year 2000

Using data from sites starting with just 5 years
6 records for each state a total of 18 records
expanded to include states with known higher WBRs for a total of 36 records

2000 https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/comparison/2000-2009/comparison2000.asp
2005 https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/comparison/2000-2009/comparison2005.asp
2010 https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/comparison/2010-2019/comparison2010.asp
2015 https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/comparison/2010-2019/comparison2015.asp
2020 https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/comparison/2020-2029/comparison2020.asp
2023 https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/comparison/2020-2029/comparison2023.asp

The year column had to be converted from number to date, may use ex. '2000' in future, or just ex. 01/01/2000
Tableau referred to WBR as Sum

The WI Weekly benefit rate has been stagnant while IL, MN and IA (it's border states) 
continue to increase substantially.
